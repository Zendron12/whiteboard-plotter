Hershey Sans 1-stroke SVG font
Source: https://gitlab.com/oskay/svg-fonts/-/raw/master/fonts/Hershey/HersheySans1.svg

Originally prepared in 2011 and converted to SVG fonts in 2019 by Windell H. Oskay,
www.evilmadscientist.com.

Contents adapted from emergent.unpythonic.net/software/hershey by way of
"Hershey Fonts in SVG" by Marty McGuire.
